About us

Rainy Days has been making and selling quality jackets since we started back in 2007. Our jackets are made from sustainable and high quality materials, which is what makes them so unique.

This adventure started in 2007 when a group of friends were out hiking. After walking through a dense forest, some of the jackets were scratched and started tearing up. The result: wet, cold and uncomfortable. That gave way for the idea of a high quality jacket that would endure dense forest as well as extreme weather, and here we are.

We like to see our customers as cooperators. You help us push our comfort zone by creating jackets suitable for your needs, and we help you push your comfort zone by making those jackets.
